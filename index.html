Soha, aap ka wujood apne aap mein ek tohfa hai,
Zindagi aapke saath aur bhi haseen lagti hai.
Aapki baatein, aapki hansi, aapka andaaz —
har cheez mein ek khaas rangeen ada hai.
🌸
Har pal aapka noor alag hi hota hai,
Dil kehta hai — kash yeh din rukk jaaye.
Aaj ke din sirf yahi duaa hai mere dil se,
Soha ki khushiyan kabhi kum na ho jaayein.
🤲 Allah Pak aapko lambi, sehatmand
aur khushiyon bhari zindagi ata farmaye.
Aapki har dua qubool ho,
har sapna pura ho.
— Ameen. 🤲
